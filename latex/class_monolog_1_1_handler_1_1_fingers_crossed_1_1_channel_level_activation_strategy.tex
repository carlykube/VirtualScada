\subsection{Detailed Description}
Channel and \doxyref{Error}{p.}{class_error} level based monolog activation strategy. Allows to trigger activation based on level per channel. e.\+g. trigger activation on level \textquotesingle{}E\+R\+R\+O\+R\textquotesingle{} by default, except for records of the \textquotesingle{}sql\textquotesingle{} channel; those should trigger activation on level \textquotesingle{}W\+A\+R\+N\textquotesingle{}.
