\subsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Pbkdf2\+Password\+Encoder}{p.}{class_symfony_1_1_component_1_1_security_1_1_core_1_1_encoder_1_1_pbkdf2_password_encoder} uses the P\+B\+K\+D\+F2 (Password-\/\+Based Key Derivation Function 2).

Providing a high level of Cryptographic security, P\+B\+K\+D\+F2 is recommended by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (N\+I\+S\+T).

But also warrants a warning, using P\+B\+K\+D\+F2 (with a high number of iterations) slows down the process. P\+B\+K\+D\+F2 should be used with caution and care.
