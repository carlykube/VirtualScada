1 # PHPUnit
2 
3 PHPUnit is a programmer-oriented testing framework for PHP. It is an instance of the xUnit architecture for unit testing frameworks.

10 ## Installation
11 
12 We distribute a [PHP Archive (PHAR)](http://php.net/phar) that has all required (as well as some optional) dependencies of PHPUnit bundled in a single file:
13 
14  wget https://phar.phpunit.de/phpunit.phar
15  chmod +x phpunit.phar
16  mv phpunit.phar /usr/local/bin/phpunit
17 
18 You can also immediately use the PHAR after you have downloaded it, of course:
19 
20  wget https://phar.phpunit.de/phpunit.phar
21  php phpunit.phar
22 
23 Alternatively, you may use [Composer](http://getcomposer.org/) to download and install PHPUnit as well as its dependencies. Please refer to the [documentation](https://phpunit.de/documentation.html) for details on how to do this.
